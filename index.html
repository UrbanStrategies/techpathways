Our Mission

A new website that helps connect young people with training to expose them to or get them jobs in the tech sector.

Career Paths

The website will feature clear job profiles that help young people and adults looking to switch sectors- these profiles will tell you all you need to know about every major job in the tech world, from coders to visual designers to desktop support- and how to get that job.

Training Opportunities

Know what career path you want? Great, our directory will show you all the places you can gain the skills you need for this career- from early stage exposure programs to whet your appetite about this new career, to job readiness certification programs to get you in the door.

Employer Support

We're also building the site to help employers find ways to support the tech ecosystem in the East Bay- from nonprofits looking for computer donations, to mentoring opportunities and volunteer needs.

Partners

TechPathways.org is a collaboration of local organizations, led by Urban Strategies Council.
